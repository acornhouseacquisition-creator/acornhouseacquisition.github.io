Florida's Unique Market Challenges

  • Property insurance premiums have doubled or tripled since 2020 in many FL counties
  • Citizens Insurance non-renewals are pushing thousands of homeowners out
  • HOA special assessments — particularly in older condos post-Surfside — can be $20,000–$100,000+
  • Flood zone reclassifications are making some properties nearly unsellable through traditional channels
  • Seasonal market swings mean South FL winter sales outperform summer by significant margins

We buy through all of these complications. None of it affects our ability to close quickly and cleanly.
