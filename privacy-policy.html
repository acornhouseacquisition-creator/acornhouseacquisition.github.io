Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026  ·  Acorn House Acquisition LLC

1. Introduction

Acorn House Acquisition LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates acornhouseacquisition.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or submit your information through our forms.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and property address when you submit a form or contact us.
  • Property Information: Details about the property you wish to sell, including address, condition, and circumstances of sale.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Communication Data: Records of calls, emails, and text messages between you and our team.

3. How We Use Your Information

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with affiliated buyers, licensed title companies, real estate attorneys, and transaction coordinators involved in the closing process, or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at info@acornhouseacquisition.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Cookies

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
