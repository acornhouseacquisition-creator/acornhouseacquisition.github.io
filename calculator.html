Common Questions
Enter the After Repair Value (ARV) of your property and the estimated repair costs. Our calculator instantly shows your estimated cash offer. The final offer is confirmed after an in-person property review and is not binding until signed by both parties.
A Subject To purchase means Acorn House Acquisition takes ownership of your property while your existing mortgage stays in place. We take over the payments going forward. This allows sellers with little or no equity to exit without bringing cash to the closing table — all closed through a licensed title company.
Seller financing means the seller acts as the lender. Instead of getting a bank loan, the buyer makes monthly payments directly to the seller. We offer flexible down payment options from 0% to 20%, with interest rates that adjust proportionally — from 5% at 0% down to 0% at 20% down.
We can close cash transactions in as little as 7 days. Subject To and seller financing transactions typically close in 14 to 21 days. We work around the seller's timeline and can accommodate faster closings for foreclosure situations.
Zero fees, zero commissions, and we cover all closing costs. The offer we make is exactly what you receive at closing — nothing deducted, nothing hidden. If you are represented by a real estate agent, we honor their commission in full.
Yes. We buy homes in any condition — no repairs needed. Foundation issues, roof damage, fire damage, deferred maintenance — leave everything as-is and we handle everything after closing. No staging, no showings, no open houses.
