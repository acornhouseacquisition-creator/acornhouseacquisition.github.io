About Acorn House Acquisition

We Buy on Your Terms — On-Market or Off.

Acorn House Acquisition is a principal real estate investment company specializing in cash and creative finance acquisitions. We buy both on-market MLS listings and off-market opportunities — whatever path gets the deal closed for the seller.

We work with homeowners who need a fast, clean exit — and with real estate agents and wholesale partners who have deals that traditional buyers can't or won't close. Our experienced Transaction Coordinator and Acquisition Managers are highly responsive and committed to smooth, efficient closings.

Every transaction we close goes through a licensed title company with a full HUD/ALTA settlement statement. We regularly work with vetted partners, investors, and title companies to ensure every deal is handled with professionalism and integrity.

Our Process

What Makes Us Easy to Work With

Whether you are a homeowner, a real estate agent, or a wholesale partner — here is exactly what working with Acorn House Acquisition looks like.

Clear Communication

We respond fast and stay in contact every step of the way. Our acquisition managers and transaction coordinator are highly responsive — no waiting, no guessing.

Clean Contracts

Straightforward purchase agreements with no unusual contingencies. We are happy to use your state's standard real estate contract if preferred.

Flexible Timelines

We close on your schedule — as fast as 7 days or as long as needed. We work around the seller's situation, not the other way around.

On-Market & Off-Market

We actively pursue MLS listings and off-market opportunities. If you have a listing or an off-market lead — bring it to us. We close both.

No Retrades

When we sign, we close at that number — no retrades without documented justification. Our reputation depends on this and we take it seriously.

Agent-Friendly

We respect agent relationships and honor commissions on every represented transaction. We structure deals creatively to make closings happen that traditional buyers can't.
